Who We Are

“He is like a tree planted beside flowing streams that bears its fruit in its season and whose leaf does not wither.” – Psalm 1:3 CSB

Mission: GrowBeyondAI exists to equip children and families to engage technology and AI with biblical wisdom, creative purpose, and a heart for discipleship.

Vision: Raising the next generation of Christ-followers—digitally wise, deeply rooted, and ready to shine.

Our journey began with one question: How can we empower families to navigate the digital age with faith-filled wisdom? In a world captivated by screens, we bridge Scripture and tech.

We teach families not only to use AI and modern tools—but to think critically through a Biblical lens. Every piece of technology, from code to chatbot, can reflect the ultimate Creator.
